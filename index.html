What Are Binary Options?

Binary options trading is a simple yet powerful way to trade on financial markets. It involves predicting whether the price of an asset (like stocks, currencies, or commodities) will go up or down within a specific time frame. If you predict correctly, you earn a fixed payout. It’s straightforward, and the potential for profit is high with the right strategy and risk management.
