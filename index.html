What is Binary Options Trading?

Binary options trading is the simplest way to trade financial markets. You only need to predict whether an asset’s price will go up or down within a set time frame. If your prediction is correct, you earn a fixed payout.

Unlike forex or stock trading, binary options allow you to start with minimal capital and require no deep financial expertise.
